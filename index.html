Meet the Gods and Goddesses of Asgard
Thor is the god of thunder and lightning. He is the son of Odin and the giantess Jörð. He is the defender of Asgard and Midgard.
Loki is the god of mischief and trickery. He is the son of the giantess Laufey and the blood brother of Odin.
Odin is the god of wisdom, poetry, and war. He is the father of Thor and the ruler of Asgard. He is the husband of Frigg and the father of Baldr.
Freyja is the goddess of love, beauty, and fertility. She is the daughter of Njörðr and the twin sister of Freyr.
Heimdall is the god of light, the son of nine mothers, and the guardian of the Bifröst. He is the watchman of the gods and the father of humankind.
